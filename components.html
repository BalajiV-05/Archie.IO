WINDOW DESIGNS

The window is also an inseparable part of the exterior because it can affect the way a home looks
from the outside. Window spacing, size, design, shape, etc., are parts of window designing. Window trim is a
way of adding excitement to your home. Always keep in mind the proper window trim design, window trim colour,
and type of window trim. To add dimension to your windows, you can choose a flared top. If you want a
contemporary design, try ornamental shutters, basic sunshade bar designs, etc.

LANDSCAPE DESIGNS

Without a proper landscape design, architectural beauty can not be completed. A good and unique landscape
elicits a good emotional vibe. You can take the help of landscape designers to create exquisite garden areas.
The landscape design includes a planned garden, coloured walkways, shrub design, flower grouping,
lighting based on plants, texture, the theme of seating areas, etc.

SUITABLE LIGHTINGS

Lighting has a significant impact on the exterior. Lighting is the symbol of a good mood and efficiency. Always try to
plan for a strategic lighting statement outside your home. Well-planned lighting can illuminate the walkways,
garden, seating area, etc.

DOOR DESIGNS

Impressive door designs can add an extra level of exterior beauty to your home. Your home can be converted into a
head-turning property if your exterior door can be the focal point of the whole exterior. Also, to complement the exterior
door, you can use bright paint to create a dramatic look. Apart from this, you may opt for a wooden shade or any
neutral colour for the door. Try something unique by adding a contrasting mailbox beside the door, a beautifully
crafted address board, a decorated nameplate, etc.

FRONT PORCH DESIGNS

The porch is the most outside place of your house, which should match the whole exterior theme. You can match
the additional seating area and lounge outside your home based on the other exterior design. It can also be a great place
to enjoy evening talk with neighbours
